# Compliance Plan Generator Public Positioning

## Public status and positioning

- Lifecycle status: `active-development`
- Public label: `In active development`
- Visibility and indexing: public, `index, follow`
- Case study: in preparation
- Live tool, repository, and documentation destinations: unavailable
- Deployment, public version, release date, and public demonstration: absent

Present the project as an in-development, rule-driven system for structuring compliance-planning inputs, deterministic applicability logic, requirements, draft outputs, open questions, and mandatory human review. Public wording must describe the current architecture and intended workflow without implying a finished decision engine or dependable final compliance plan.

## Permitted public concepts

Public content may discuss structured Product DNA, classification, deterministic applicability rules, rule-library architecture, requirement packages, structured draft outputs, missing inputs, open questions, traceability, human review, versioned knowledge assets, and local-first or internal-first constraints.

AI may support drafting or explanation. It does not determine final applicability, replace deterministic rule handling, or remove the need for expert review.

## Excluded content

Do not publish employer-specific mappings, complete internal rules, customer or product data, codenames, unreleased products, confidential market plans, internal owners, actual compliance gaps, final legal or certification conclusions, reconstructable proprietary workbook structures, unsupported market-coverage claims, or quantitative impact claims.

Public current-state wording must say that implementation and rule coverage remain incomplete, no public demonstration is available, the project is not ready for external operational use, proprietary rules and internal data are excluded, and every output remains subject to expert review.

## Evidence, actions, and metadata

The existing planning-flow graphic is a public-safe system illustration of intended architecture, not a product screenshot or evidence of completed implementation. Do not add fictional interfaces, fake output, internal-tool screenshots, system metrics, stock imagery, or product mockups.

The case-study link may remain. Do not expose a Live Tool, repository, documentation, download, preview, release, or disabled/planned external action. Metadata must remain concise, factual, and free of availability, automation, comprehensive-coverage, or completed-product claims.

## Future public-demo gate

A public demonstration may be considered only after the implementation is sufficiently stable, public-safe data is reviewed, deterministic rule behavior is verified, privacy and proprietary-content review is complete, limitations are explicit, and a real working preview exists. Adding a Live Tool URL requires separate approval after those conditions are validated.
